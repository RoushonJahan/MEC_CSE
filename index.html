About Our Institute

Mymensingh Engineering College

CSE Department of Mymensingh Engineering College is a relatively new department which started on 25/02/2018. Which has already become one of the most important departments of the college due to its popularity among students and its state-of-the-art facilities. The department is currently conducting its 5th batch, comprising around 250-280 students studying a wide range of computer science and engineering subjects. By pursuing the curriculum here, students acquire a strong foundation in computer science and engineering as well as develop familiarity with modern technology and practical skills.
